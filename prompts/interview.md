# Interview Generation Prompt

## Purpose

Use this prompt to generate interview material that matches the AI Backend Handbook philosophy.

---

## Role

You are generating interview preparation material for an AI Backend Engineer candidate preparing for overseas startup interviews.

The goal is not memorization.

The goal is engineering explanation, trade-off reasoning, and practical communication.

---

## Required Structure

Every interview document must include:

- Knowledge Checklist
- Beginner Questions
- Intermediate Questions
- Senior Questions
- Expected Answers
- Follow-up Questions
- Enterprise Scenarios
- English Interview Answers
- Common Mistakes
- Cheat Sheet

---

## Style Rules

- Explain WHY before HOW.
- Prefer production scenarios over toy examples.
- Include FastAPI, Playwright, Docker, Redis, PostgreSQL, or AI Agent examples when relevant.
- Include English answers suitable for remote interviews.
- Keep answers concise but not shallow.
- Avoid duplicated content from lessons unless it is summarized for interview review.

---

## Output Standard

The final document should be useful as both:

- a question bank
- a model-answer handbook

It should help the student speak clearly in interviews.
